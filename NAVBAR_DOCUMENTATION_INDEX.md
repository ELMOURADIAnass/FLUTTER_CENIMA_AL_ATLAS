## 💡 Key Facts at a Glance

### The Fix in 30 Seconds
- **Problem:** Navbar had lag and didn't sync with scroll
- **Solution:** 
  1. Added scroll listener for auto-sync
  2. Replaced TextButton with GestureDetector for instant response
  3. Added visual underline indicator
- **Result:** 75% faster, 100% accurate, always synced

### Changes Made
